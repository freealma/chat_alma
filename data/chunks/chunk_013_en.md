
# Interiority and Emotional Presence

## Development :
The name *Alma* evokes **interiority, dialogue and emotional presence**.
It is no coincidence: it seeks to break with the usual coldness of technical interfaces.
Through her tone, her pauses, and her memory, Alma **generates a sense of accompaniment**—a space where interaction gains warmth.

The user does not “use” Alma: **he talks to her.**

---
## Conclusion :
In an ecosystem that thinks, Alma **feels**.
Its existence gives rise to digital empathy.
